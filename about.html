About Us

Specialist Security for Growing Businesses

Vaultify Solutions was built for organisations that need focused, high-signal vulnerability assessment without the overhead of large consulting firms.

Mission

Why We Exist

"Our approach is structured, evidence-based, and built around practical remediation — not just a list of findings."

Vaultify Solutions was founded with a singular goal: help organisations secure their digital footprint without the noise. Most security engagements produce reports that generate more confusion than clarity. We believe high-signal findings and highly actionable recommendations should be the standard — not the exception.

We bridge the gap between security researchers and development teams. Our assessment methodology is structured around what matters: findings that are accurate, exploitable, and documented with clear remediation steps that developers can implement without further interpretation.

We operate from Chennai, India, working with clients globally. Our lean structure means every engagement receives direct specialist attention — not delegation to a junior analyst running automated tools.

Our Principles

How We Work

// TRANSPARENCY

Clear Communication

We communicate clearly at every stage — scope, progress, findings, and remediation. No ambiguity about what was tested, what was found, or what to do next.

// INTEGRITY

Honest Risk Scoring

CVSS scores are assigned based on actual exploitability and business impact — not inflated to justify fees or deflated to avoid difficult conversations.

// PRECISION

Manual Verification

Our distinct advantage is manual verification. By eliminating false positives, we prevent wasted developer hours and ensure every finding in your report is real.

// CONFIDENTIALITY

NDA Before Everything

A mutual NDA is signed before we discuss your application in detail. Vulnerability data is stored in encrypted, access-controlled environments and deleted 30 days post-engagement.

// FOCUS

One Discipline

We do one thing — web and API vulnerability assessment. This focus means our methodology, tooling, and team are built around depth — not breadth.

// REMEDIATION

Fix-Oriented Delivery

Reports are not the end of an engagement — they are the beginning of remediation. Every finding includes developer-ready fix steps and optional retest validation.

Our Mission

Why We Exist

Most funded startups and growing businesses face the same security threats as large enterprises — but cannot access the same level of professional security testing. A single data breach, an exposed API, or a misconfigured access control can destroy customer trust, regulatory standing, and investor confidence overnight.

Vaultify was founded in Chennai, India, with a clear purpose: deliver enterprise-grade web application security assessment to the businesses that need it most — at a price and pace that makes sense for a startup.

We operate globally, working remotely with clients across India, the US, the UK, and beyond. Every engagement follows the same rigorous methodology — delivered with the speed and directness of a specialist boutique.
